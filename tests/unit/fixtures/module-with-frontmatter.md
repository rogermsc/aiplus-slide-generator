# Data Privacy Fundamentals

## Overview

Data privacy is the practice of handling personal data in compliance with regulations and ethical standards.

## GDPR vs CCPA

| Feature | GDPR | CCPA |
|---------|------|------|
| Region | EU | California |
| Scope | All organizations | Revenue > $25M |
| Right to delete | Yes | Yes |
| Penalties | Up to 4% revenue | Up to $7,500/violation |
