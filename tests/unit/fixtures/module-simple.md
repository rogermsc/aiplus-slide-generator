# Introduction to AI Ethics

## What is AI Ethics?

AI ethics is the branch of ethics that examines the moral implications of artificial intelligence systems. It covers fairness, accountability, transparency, and privacy.

- Fairness in algorithmic decision-making
- Accountability for AI outcomes
- Transparency in model behavior
- Privacy protection in data usage

## Key Principles

### Fairness

Fairness requires that AI systems do not discriminate based on protected characteristics such as race, gender, or age.

### Transparency

Organizations must be able to explain how their AI systems make decisions. This is often called "explainability" or "interpretability."
