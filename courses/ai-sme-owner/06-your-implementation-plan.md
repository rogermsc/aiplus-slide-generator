What AI CAN Do:
- Analyze business needs
- Create customized plans
- Suggest appropriate tools
- Provide templates
- Guide setup processes

What AI CANNOT Do:
- Make you start using tools
- Replace business judgment
- Handle relationship management
- Navigate company culture
- Force follow-through

Key Insight: AI creates the perfect plan - execution is still up to you. Success depends on taking action, not perfect planning.
